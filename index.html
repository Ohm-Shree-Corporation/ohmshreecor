About us

Ohm Shree Corporation is the registered and established company operating since 1967, dealing in various parts and accessories for the Cotton Ginning Industry. Our office and Warehouse are situated In Nadiad, Gujarat India. We are specialized in spare parts for Double Roller Ginning Machines. we are Dealing, Supplying and Exporting all parts of Double Roller Gin manufactured machines. We have high quality and vigorously tested parts made of optimum quality of raw materials. We supply a wide range of Cotton Ginning Machinery Spare Parts, Double Roller Ginning Machine Spare Parts of Bajaj and Jadhao, Ginning Machine Oil Seal, Cotton Hydraulic Press and its part, Ginning Machine Gear Box products, All type of ball bearing, Complete Pre Cleaner and its parts, Auto Feeder and its Parts, All type nute and bolts, Super lint cleaner and its parts in India and abroad, we have been working with and supplying various ginning industries. The supplied parts and accessories are of high quality to customer satisfaction.
