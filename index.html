About us

Om Shree Corporation is the registered and established company operating since 1967, dealing in various parts and accessories for the Cotton Ginning Industry. Our office and Warehouse are situated In Nadiad, Gujarat India. We are specialized in spare parts for Double Roller Ginning Machines. we are Dealing, Supplying and Exporting all parts of Double Roller Gin manufactured machines. We have high quality and vigorously tested parts made of optimum quality of raw materials. We supply a wide range of Cotton Ginning Machinery Spare Parts, Double Roller Ginning Machine Spare Parts, Ginning Machine Oil Seal, Cotton Hydraulic Press Parts and Ginning Machine Gear Box products, All type of ball bearing in the market. Complete Pre Cleaner and parts, Auto Feeder & Parts, Super Lint Cleaner Parts. In India and abroad, we have been working with and supplying various ginning industries. The supplied parts and accessories are of high quality to customer satisfaction.
